Who We Are

About Us

The Western Mass Bitcoin Meetup is on a mission to find and connect bitcoiners in the area and has been growing quickly.

We are interested in spreading education, increasing local merchant adoption and hanging out with bitcoiners. We meet once a month and all are welcome to come and talk bitcoin.

If you are new, we have members who can help you level up your knowledge related to self-custody, lightning, mining and many other areas.
