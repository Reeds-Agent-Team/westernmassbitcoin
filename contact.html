Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the next meetup?
All of our upcoming meetups are listed on our Events page.
Can I come if I don't know anything about Bitcoin?
Yes! We cover material that is accessible to anyone, even if you are new to bitcoin. Email us at WMBTCM@protonmail.com if you have any questions before attending.
Do I have to RSVP?
We prefer to have an estimate of how many people will attend. RSVP is preferred but not required.
Is there a cost to attend?
No, it is free to join our meetups. Food and drinks are available for purchase at the venue.
